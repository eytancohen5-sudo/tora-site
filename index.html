The Concept

Not a hotel. Not quite a villa.

A villa-hotel, scattered like a village — villa rooms, suite-villas and whole villas across the most beautiful corner of Antiparos. There is a lobby and a welcome; everything else is drawn for privacy. Each building is designed so that every guest, in a single villa room or an entire villa, keeps their own walls, their own quiet, their own view. You arrive at one place and live across many.

Antiparos — the quiet muse

A stay is the beginning. Creativity is the rest.

TΩRA is a home for creatives — creativity is the backbone of the house, present everywhere, forced nowhere. Artists are in residence through the season; guests keep their own distance from the making, which we call freedom of proximity — watch, join in, create, or do nothing at all. Around it runs a quiet dialogue with Antiparos and its makers. That exchange, between artist, guest and island, is what you will find nowhere else.
